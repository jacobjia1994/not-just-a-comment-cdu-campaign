What gets passed on

Silence teaches the room too.

When nobody responds, the person targeted may be left to absorb it, explain it, or decide whether the space is safe anymore.

Tap the chain

Small moments do not stay small when they keep happening.

First Nations students should not have to carry the comment, explain the harm, and teach the room.
