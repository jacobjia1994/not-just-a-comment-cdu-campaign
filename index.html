60-second response practice

Pick one line you could actually say.

There is no perfect speech under pressure. The goal is to pause the pattern, reduce harm and avoid making the person targeted carry the moment.

Five everyday moments

Short enough to remember. Useful enough to use.

"It was only a joke."

If identity is the punchline, pause it.

"They get special treatment."

Equity is not a favour.

"I do not see race."

Racism does.

"Can you explain the Aboriginal view?"

One student is not your reading list.

"No one reported it."

Silence is not proof.

After the click

Do one useful thing after the moment.

Check in privately. Do not ask the person harmed to reassure you. Do not force a complaint. If you need options, use the support page.
