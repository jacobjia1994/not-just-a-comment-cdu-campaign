If this happened to you

You do not have to decide everything at once.

Support can mean talking with someone, writing down what happened, asking about options or making a report. You should have as much control as possible over what happens next.

1

I want to talk to someone.

Start with wellbeing support, especially if the incident is sitting in your body, affecting sleep, study, trust or safety.

CDU life, health and wellbeing
2

I want reporting options.

You can ask about options before deciding what to do. A complaint should not become another pressure point.

CDU complaints information
3

I am worried about immediate safety.

If there is immediate danger, call 000. If you are overwhelmed or in crisis, contact crisis support now.
